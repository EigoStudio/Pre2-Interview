No. 4: Your Opinion

Q: Do you think more schools will use AI for English lessons in the future?

Hint: convenient / ask questions / save time / smart assistant
1. I think AI is very convenient for students when they have questions.
2. AI gives answers very quickly, so students don't have to wait.
3. Teachers can save a lot of time because AI is like a smart assistant for them.
Hint: talking with teachers / more important / face-to-face / human connection
1. I think talking with teachers face-to-face is more important for students.
2. AI sometimes gives wrong information, so it is safer to learn from teachers.
3. Teachers can understand students' feelings better than AI can.

No. 5: Your Opinion

Q: Do you want to use AI when you travel to foreign countries?

Hint: easy to talk / local people / understand signs
1. Because it is easy to talk to local people using AI devices.
2. AI can help me understand foreign signs and menus quickly.
3. I think it is very useful because I don't have to carry a heavy dictionary.
Hint: study harder / my own words / make friends
1. Because I want to speak with my own words to make friends.
2. I want to study English harder by myself without using AI.
3. Talking without AI is more exciting and fun when I travel.
