No. 4: Your Opinion

Q: Do you think more schools will use AI for English lessons in the future?

Hint: convenient / ask questions / quickly
1. I think AI is very convenient for students when they study.
2. AI gives answers very quickly, so students don't have to wait.
3. It is a good way to learn new things in a fun way.
Hint: teachers / face-to-face / human connection
1. I think talking with teachers face-to-face is more important.
2. AI sometimes makes mistakes, so it is safer to learn from teachers.
3. Teachers can understand students' feelings better than AI can.

No. 5: Your Opinion

Q: Do you want to use AI when you travel to foreign countries?

Hint: easy to talk / local people / understand signs
1. It is easy to talk to local people using AI devices.
2. AI can help me understand foreign signs and menus quickly.
3. I don't have to carry a heavy dictionary when I travel.
Hint: own words / make friends / study harder
1. I want to speak with my own words to make friends.
2. I want to study English harder by myself without using AI.
3. Talking without AI is more exciting and fun for me.
